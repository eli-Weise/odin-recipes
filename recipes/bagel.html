Bagel with Lox and Cream Cheese

Description

For the standard (if slightly lacking on some ingredients) way to eat a bagel

Ingredients

  • Bagel of your choice
  • Cream cheese
  • Lox

Steps

  1. Slice bagel in half perpendicular to the hole
  2. Add a reasonable amount of cream cheese to both sides of the bagel
  3. Add desired amount of lox, making sure to evenly distribute it without covering the hole or overhanging to edge
